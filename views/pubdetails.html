Frequently Asked Questions
Are your prices negotiable?

We love data. Our pricing is driven by data as we take into account what cars within the same parameters (year, make, model, trim, mileage) would sell around the area. Our prices are always below average dealership list prices. We don’t negotiate, up or down, and we’re very transparent about it all.

When pricing cars, our team takes into consideration the retail, auction, private party and other markets in any given region. If the price does ever drop, we’ll be sure to notify you, but cannot guarantee that the car will still be available in the future.

Can I see multiple cars?

If you are looking to compare multiple cars in Haladrive’s inventory, we invite you to come to our parking hub for your appointment so that you can see them both at the same time. To schedule one of these appointments, please call us at 855-744-3801

Is this car still available?

Our website is updated live. As long as the car is not listed as “Coming Soon”, “Sale Pending”, or “Sold” it is available for test drives.

Does Haladrive provide warranties?

Every Shift Certified car comes with a 30-day limited warranty and a 5-day return guarantee. In addition to the included warranty, Shift offers vehicle protection plans that safeguard you from the unexpected costs of car ownership. Our protection plans are backed by industry-leading partners and provide total coverage against many mechanical and electrical problems you may face. Learn more

Do I need an appointment?

Yes! To ensure your vehicle is available and a driver is assigned to you, we require booking a test drive appointment in advance. Book your test drive today!
